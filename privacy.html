Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

🔒 Private use only — no data sharing

Overview

BudgetLogger is a personal budgeting tool built for private use only. This app is not a commercial product and is not intended for use by the general public.

All financial data entered into BudgetLogger belongs solely to the individual user and is never shared with, sold to, or accessible by any third party.

Data storage

  • All budget data is stored exclusively in your own Google Sheets spreadsheet, which only you can access.
  • No data is stored on any external server, database, or cloud service operated by this app.
  • OAuth credentials (access tokens, refresh tokens) are stored locally on your device only, using your browser's localStorage.
  • App preferences (dark mode, notification settings) are stored locally on your device only.

Google account access

BudgetLogger uses Google OAuth 2.0 to access your Google Sheets spreadsheet. The app requests the minimum permissions necessary:

  • Read and write access to one specific Google Sheets spreadsheet (the one you provide the ID for).
  • No access to your email, contacts, calendar, other files, or any other Google services.
  • Your Google credentials are never transmitted to or stored by any server other than Google's own OAuth servers.

Data we do not collect

  • No analytics or usage tracking of any kind.
  • No advertising, no ad tracking, no third-party trackers.
  • No personal identifiers, IP addresses, or device information.
  • No cookies beyond what is strictly necessary for authentication.

Intended use

This application is intended exclusively for private personal use by the individual who configured and deployed it. It is not designed or authorised for use by multiple users, organisations, or members of the public.

If you have accessed this application without the explicit permission of the owner, please discontinue use immediately.

Contact

This app is privately operated. For questions about your data, please contact the individual who deployed this application directly.
